1. Cancellation Overview

FreshKart understands that circumstances may require order cancellation. This policy outlines the conditions under which orders can be cancelled and the applicable refund process.

2. Customer Cancellation - Allowed

  • Orders in "Pending" status can be cancelled at any time
  • Cancellation must be done before order is dispatched for delivery
  • Cancellation available through My Orders section on website
  • Cancellation via WhatsApp +91 7888716909 also accepted

3. Customer Cancellation - Not Allowed

  • Orders that are already out for delivery cannot be cancelled
  • Orders marked as "Delivered" cannot be cancelled
  • Partial cancellation of items within an order is not available

4. How to Cancel Your Order

Method 1 - Website: Login → My Orders → Select Order → Click Cancel Order

Method 2 - WhatsApp: Send your Order ID to +91 7888716909

Method 3 - Email: Send Order ID to sk3209267@gmail.com

5. Cancellation by FreshKart

FreshKart reserves the right to cancel orders under the following circumstances:

  • Products out of stock or unavailable
  • Delivery not feasible to provided address
  • Suspected fraudulent transaction detected
  • Incorrect pricing due to technical error
  • Natural calamities or force majeure events

In all such cases, full refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

6. Refund on Cancellation

  • Cancelled before processing: Full refund within 5-7 business days
  • Cancelled by FreshKart: Full refund within 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Wallet cancellations: Refund within 2-3 business days
  • Card/Net Banking cancellations: Refund within 5-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery cancellations: No payment refund applicable

7. Cancellation Confirmation

Upon successful cancellation, you will receive a confirmation message via WhatsApp and email. The cancellation status will also be updated in your My Orders section on the website.
