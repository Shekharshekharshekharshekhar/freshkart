1. Refund Overview

At FreshKart, customer satisfaction is our priority. We offer refunds for eligible cases as described in this policy. All refund requests are processed promptly and fairly.

2. Eligible Refund Cases

  • Wrong items delivered (different from what was ordered)
  • Damaged, spoiled, or rotten products delivered
  • Order not delivered but payment was deducted
  • Duplicate payment charged for same order
  • Order cancelled before dispatch
  • Significant quality issues with delivered products

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after successful delivery
  • Minor natural variations in fresh produce (size, shape, color)
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by customer
  • Customer unavailable at delivery location
  • Refund request made after 24 hours of delivery

7. Partial Refunds
